This template provides a comprehensive form for creating documents formatted according to the American Psychological Association (APA) 7th edition style guidelines. It supports both student and professional papers. Key features include dynamic management of authors and their affiliations (including ORCID iDs), automatic formatting of cover pages, abstracts, and keywords. The project structure organizes your work into sections, assets, and bibliography files. Ideal for research papers, essays, and academic articles requiring strict adherence to APA standards without the hassle of manual formatting.
